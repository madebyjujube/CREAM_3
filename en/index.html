After a year-long hiatus we’re back(!) with the fifth edition of C.R.E.A.M. Join us on Friday August 29th 2025 as we pay homage to our hero and our heartbeat: David Lynch. Hailing from the birthplace of Lynch himself (Missoula, Montana), we are ecstatic to welcome identical twin performance artists MOTHERMARY, followed by a screening of “WILD AT HEART” and a rave to seal the eve with a kiss. The NEW YORK REVIEW OF SMUT will also be launching the first edition of their nasty piece of work, with copies for sale all night long.


With support from Inner Circle. We will be raising funds for local organization ASTT(e)Q at the bar.

About WILD AT HEART:
Desire and danger collide in this feverish masterpiece, chronicling two passionate young lovers Sailor (Nicolas Cage) and Lula (Laura Dean) as they hit the road to escape the iron grip of her overbearing mother and a slew of grotesque characters along the way. This freewheeling, primal howl of a film is both a chaotic love letter to rebellion and a nightmarish descent into the American subconscious. Sensual, visceral and completely off the rails, noir meets romance meets absurdity with Lynch’s signature dream-logic guiding the way.
